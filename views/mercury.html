Welcome to Mercury!



Mercury is the closest planet to the sun in the solar system. It does not have an atmosphere, or any moons that orbit around the planet. In terms of size it is the smallest planet in the solar system at 4879km in diamater. An interesting fact about Mercury is that one day on the planet is equivalent to 176 days on Earth.
